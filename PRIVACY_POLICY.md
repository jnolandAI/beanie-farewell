# Bean Bye Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2026**

## Overview

Bean Bye ("the App") is a mobile application that helps users identify and value their Beanie Baby collectibles using AI-powered image recognition. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Camera and Photo Access
- The App accesses your device's camera and photo library solely to capture or select images of Beanie Babies for identification.
- Photos are sent to our AI service for analysis and are **not stored** on our servers after processing.
- We do not retain, share, or use your photos for any purpose other than providing the identification service.

### Collection Data (Local Only)
- Your scanned Beanie Baby collection is stored **locally on your device** using secure storage.
- This data includes: item names, estimated values, thumbnail images, and scan timestamps.
- Collection data is **never transmitted** to our servers or any third parties.
- You can delete your collection data at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the App.

### User-Provided Information
- If you choose to provide your name during onboarding, it is stored **locally on your device** for personalization of Farewell Certificates.
- This information is never transmitted to our servers.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- **No personal identification information** (email, phone, address)
- **No account creation required** - the App works without sign-up
- **No analytics or tracking** - we do not track your usage patterns
- **No location data**
- **No advertising identifiers**
- **No third-party analytics SDKs**

## AI Image Processing

When you scan a Beanie Baby:
1. The image is sent securely to our AI service (via encrypted HTTPS connection)
2. The AI analyzes the image and returns identification results
3. The image is immediately discarded after processing
4. No images are stored on our servers

## Data Security

- All network communications are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- Local data is stored using your device's secure storage mechanisms
- We implement rate limiting to prevent abuse of our services

## Children's Privacy

Bean Bye does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is intended for nostalgic millennials looking to value their childhood collectibles.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses AI services provided by Anthropic (Claude) for image recognition. Their privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy at https://www.anthropic.com/privacy.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored locally on your device
- **Delete**: Remove all local data by clearing app data or uninstalling
- **Control**: Choose whether to use camera, photo library, or provide your name

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

## Summary

**TL;DR**: Your photos are processed for identification only and immediately discarded. Your collection stays on your device. We don't track you, don't require accounts, and don't collect personal information. Your Beanie Babies' secrets are safe with us.
