Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Overview

Beanie Farewell is a mobile application that helps users identify and value their Beanie Baby collectibles using AI-powered image recognition. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Camera and Photo Access

  • The App accesses your camera and photo library solely to capture or select images of Beanie Babies for identification.
  • Photos are sent to our AI service for analysis and are not stored on our servers after processing.
  • We do not retain, share, or use your photos for any purpose other than providing the identification service.

Collection Data (Local Only)

  • Your scanned Beanie Baby collection is stored locally on your device using secure storage.
  • This data includes: item names, estimated values, thumbnail images, and scan timestamps.
  • Collection data is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

User-Provided Information

  • If you choose to provide your name during onboarding, it is stored locally on your device for personalization.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal identification information (email, phone, address)
  • No account creation required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No location data
  • No advertising identifiers

AI Image Processing

  1. The image is sent securely to our AI service (via encrypted HTTPS)
  2. The AI analyzes the image and returns identification results
  3. The image is immediately discarded after processing
  4. No images are stored on our servers

Data Security

  • All network communications are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Local data is stored using your device secure storage mechanisms
  • We implement rate limiting to prevent abuse

Third-Party Services

The App uses AI services provided by Anthropic (Claude) for image recognition. Their privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy.

Your Rights

  • Access: View all data stored locally on your device
  • Delete: Remove all local data by clearing app data or uninstalling
  • Control: Choose whether to use camera, photo library, or provide your name

TL;DR

Your photos are processed for identification only and immediately discarded. Your collection stays on your device. We do not track you, require accounts, or collect personal information.
